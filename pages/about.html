What's poke?

Poke is a traditional Hawaiian dish that typically consists of diced raw fish (such as tuna or salmon) marinated in soy sauce and other flavorful ingredients. It is often served over a bed of rice and topped with various toppings like seaweed,
cucumber, avocado, and sesame seeds.

Traditional Heritage,Modern Result

One of the reasons for poke's popularity is its versatility. While the traditional version remains a favorite, there are now numerous variations available, allowing people to customize their poke bowls according to their preferences.
